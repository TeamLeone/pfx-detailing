Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 6, 2026

1. Company Information

This Privacy Policy describes how Leone Capital LLC, doing business as PureFX ("Company", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you visit our website or use our services, including PureFX Mobile Detailing and PureFX Small Business Consulting.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following personal information when you fill out a quote, book an appointment, fill out our application form, or otherwise interact with our services:

  • First and last name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Service location and ZIP code
  • Vehicle type, year, and condition information
  • Business type, size, and revenue range (consulting only)
  • Business challenges and goals (consulting only)

We also collect data automatically through advertising platforms including Meta (Facebook/Instagram), TikTok, and Google when you interact with our ads.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Contact you regarding your detailing appointment or Discovery Call booking
  • Deliver our mobile detailing and consulting services
  • Send confirmation and reminder emails through Go High Level
  • Improve our marketing and advertising campaigns
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Third Party Services

We use the following third party platforms that may process your data:

  • Go High Level — CRM, email automation, and calendar booking
  • FastPayDirect — Payment processing for the $10 Discovery Call fee
  • Meta (Facebook/Instagram) — Advertising and lead tracking
  • TikTok — Advertising and lead tracking
  • Google — Advertising and lead tracking

Each of these platforms has their own privacy policy governing how they handle your data.

5. Cookies and Tracking

Our website and advertising platforms use cookies and tracking pixels including the Meta Pixel, TikTok Pixel, and Google Tag to measure ad performance and optimize our campaigns. By using our website you consent to this tracking.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We only share your data with the service providers listed above as necessary to deliver our services.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to deliver our services and comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time

9. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. All payment information is processed securely through FastPayDirect.

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

12. Contact

For questions regarding this Privacy Policy contact Leone Capital LLC dba PureFX at contact@pfxdetailing.com or (404) 999-3528.
